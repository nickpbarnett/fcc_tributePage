A bit about Tim

  • Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee OM KBE FRS FREng FRSA FBCS (born 8 June 1955).
  • Also known as TimBL, is an English engineer and computer scientist, best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web.
  • He is currently a Professor of Computer Science at the University of Oxford.
  • He was knighted by Queen Elizabeth II in the 2004 New Year Honours "for services to the global development of the internet".
